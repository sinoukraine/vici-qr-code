Camera Functions
  • 1. Is my dash camera on?

    If the RED indicator light is flashing the DC100 is powered on. If you are unable to see the red light and voice prompts are activated move your hand under the camera to trigger the GESTURE mode and you will hear the photo being taken and stored.

  • 2. Indicator light meanings

    The RED indicator light will flash when the camera is powered on. The GREEN light will become solid when the DC100 is connected to WIFI. Both lights will be solid when the DC100 is updating its firmware.

  • 3. DC100 operating temperature

    It is normal for the DC100 to feel warm during operation as the internal radiation fan expels heat from the camera. The normal operating temperature is ~40 degrees Celsius.

  • 4. Voice recordings and settings

    The DC100 is able to record noise during its use. This setting is able to be turned on or off within the camera settings.

  • 5. Firmware upgrades

    The DC100 is able to have the firmware upgraded “over the air” once the DC100 is connected to a mobile phone and an upgrade is available.

  • 6. Voltage specifications

    The DC100’s power supply will accept 12 or 24V power and output 5V to the camera for safe operation.
