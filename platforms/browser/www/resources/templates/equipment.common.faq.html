Equipment Common
  • 1. How to know DVR is on work or not?

    When the red light flickers it means the DVR is recording normally. If it is not convenient to check the red light during driving, able to capture image by placing hand below device and touching shuffer sencor, instantly capturing and storing image, and if arise the voice of shooting pictures, it means the DVR is on working.

  • 2. What's the implication of the indicator lights?

    When the red light flickers it means the DVR is recording videos normally. When the green light is constantly on it means the DVR has successfully connected with mobile. When both lights are constantly on it means the DVR is upgrading its firmware version.

  • 3. Why the DVR feels hot when you touch it during using?

    DVR adopts heat radiation fan internally, out of its both sides, to ensure the main panel stability. It has obtained the National Quality Inspection Report. All car DVRs have gone through the high-and-low temperature tests. The normal working temperature of the DVR is around 38-40C, and it is normal phenomenon that it gets hot after long time using.

  • 4. Can the DVR record audios, and turn on/off recording audios?

    The DVR has the audio recording function, and after the mobile connected with it, the function of turn on/off recording audios can be set up in APP.

  • 5. Can the DVR firmware be upgraded, and how?

    Yes the DVR firmware can be upgraded. When the new firmware is available to upgrade, our server would automatically send it to the APP of dashcam. During the upgrading process it is a must that the DVR and the mobile be connected all the time, otherwise the upgrading will fail and the DVR would not work properly. Also, the power supply can not be cut off during upgrading. The whole upgrading process will complete in 2 minutes.

  • 6. What's the currency of the car charger?

    Out on-board car changer 12-24V, DC 1.5A currency input, and 5V 2.1A output. The two USB ports are self-adjusted. And the max output currency is 2.1A
