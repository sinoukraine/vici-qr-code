Dash Camera Connection
  • 1. Connecting the DC100 to the APP

    The DC100 connects to the app via the WIFI connection with your mobile device. To connect enter your WIFI settings, select the appropriate camera and click connect to enter the default password (12345678).

  • 2. APP updates

    Connect to your app store and search ATGA to manually look for updates, or alternatively if automatic updates are activated the app will update when they are available.

  • 3. Mobile data when connected to DC100

    IOS products will allow for the use of mobile data when the DC100 is connected via the APP. Android system do not allow for mobile data usage when the DC100 is connected to the mobile device.

  • 4. Downloading photos or videos

    a) Select the “tick” on the PHOTO or VIDEO gallery

    b) Check the box in the top left corner of the photo or video

    c) Select the DOWNLOAD icon in the top left-hand corner of the screen

    Photo’s and video’s will be saved to your gallery.
