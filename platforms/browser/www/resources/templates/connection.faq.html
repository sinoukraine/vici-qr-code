Connection
  • 1. How does the APP connect the DVR?

    After the DVR is installed in position and the power is through, the red light will flicker. Turn on APP, The IOS user please turn on WIFI initiated with APP set menu, and connect to it with initial password 12345678. Back to APP after connection to WIFI, then the live broadcast will display, which means the connection is done. At the mean time, the green light of the DVR will light up. And for Android users, turn of the APP, then click ‘+’ at homepage so as to add the DVR. Following the instructions, check out whether the red light will flicker or not. After that, you can find an equipment initiated with APP, click to connect. After 10 seconds or so the connection will be done, then the APP will be redirected to the live broadcasting interface, and the red light of the DVR will light up, which means the connection has been done.

  • 2. How to update APP?

    Turn on APP (keep the mobile accessable to networks, connect to WIFI or GPRS/3G/4G), click ‘Individual’ ------ ‘About’ ------ ‘APP Version’. If there is new version released, then click ‘APP Version’ and you will find a tip about the new version release and it will ask you whether to update or not. Click ‘Update’, then the APP will be updated automatically. Or, the update can be done on the application platforms such as Application Treasure.

  • 3. Whether the phone can access to the Internet while the APP is connected to the DVR?

    When the APP is connected to the DVR, for IOS system, it is still available to surf on the Internet through 3G/4G GPRS, but Android system it is not available. Android users can only surf on Internet after disconnection of the WIFI of the the DVR.

  • 4. How to download videos and pictures through APP?

    For video downloading: after connecting the DVR, keep the DVR on line, then enter into the videos album, and select the videos to be downloaded. For pictures synchronization: after connecting the DVR, keep the DVR on line, then enter into the videos album, and drop down for synchronization of pictures and relating videos to mobile.

  • 5. Wifi can not be found/connected or connect slowly while App is connecting to car camera?

    1：Car camera's WiFi will be effected by part of bluetooth device, we suggest turning off cell phone/car bluetooth device when connect to car camera.

    2：Setting-Turn off WLAN to restart/ Or restart mobile phone/car dash camera.

    a)When part of Samsung /Huawei mobile phones open mobile dates and WLAN at the same time,if WIFI signal is not good,it will change to mobile date automatically.That's why mobile phone can not connect to device successfully.

    b)Samsung phone:Now need to-Setting-WLAN-Advanced setting-Turn off Intelligent Network Switching/network switching Automaticlly You can also turn off mobile date directly.

    c)Huawei phone: Setting-WLAN-Advanced setting-Turn off "avoid weak signal network" you can also turn off mobile date directly.In other way,you can input #o011# in keypad to open ServICeMode mode,then click "more"-WIFI-Turn off wifi sacing mode-Restart mobile phone.

    3：Cell phone also can't connect to car camera if cell phone with proxy setting. You can cancel proxy according to this way- Click car camera's hotspot- Choose"Advance setting"- Proxy server-choose "no" and then save this option.
