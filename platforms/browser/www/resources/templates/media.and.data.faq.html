Media and Data
  • 1. Gesture range

    The gesture range of the DC100 is up to 10cm below the camera. By swiping your hand under the camera the image will be captured and saved within the GESTURE gallery.

  • 2. Saving images or videos

    The DC100 will automatically save some images and videos for events or incidents. All photos or videos can be downloaded to your mobile device, see DASH CAM CONNECTION FAQ. The SD card can also be inserted into a PC for image/video retrieval.

  • 3. Does the APP use data?

    The WIFI connection is a Point-to-point private connection and does not consume data, the camera should only be attempted to be connected to within a 10m range. This method of connection is chosen to increase data transfer between the DC100 and mobile device.
